About Me

Health Officer

Greetings, I am Meron Tesfaye, also known as Kiya, and I extend a warm welcome to you at 19Film Production. As the owner of 19Film Production, I actively engage in screenplay and medical script writing within the company. Additionally, I currently serve as the Deputy Chief Executive Officer for Entertainment and Events at ET Health Alley. Simultaneously, I play a pivotal role in the Ministry of Health's HIV/AIDS prevention and treatment program, contributing to the documentary "Efoyta," which sheds light on hospital services. Whether hosting the Ministry of Health TV program or acting in various dramas, I bring a dynamic blend of skills to the screen. Holding a degree in Public Health from Adama University, I offer a grounded perspective on health topics explored on this platform. Beyond the digital realm, I have prior experience working in hospitals and health centers. Feel free to explore my diverse portfolio, and I sincerely thank you for visiting 19Film Production.
